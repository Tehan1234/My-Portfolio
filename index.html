About Me

Hello I'm Anupa Tehan, a Full-Stack Developer with a strong interest in creating creative and user-friendly digital solutions. I have a solid background in both front-end and back-end development, and my area of expertise is creating dynamic apps with cutting-edge tools like Java, Angular, and Spring Boot. I developed my skills in developing responsive interfaces, troubleshooting intricate problems, and producing high-caliber features utilising React, Angular, and JavaScript while working as a Trainee Developer at ICET. Additionally, I have expertise working productively in Agile environments with Jira and Azure. In addition to my technical abilities, I am motivated by creativity and a dedication to resolving practical problems. I enjoy providing user-focused solutions, whether I'm creating a student administration system, a hotel booking system, or a weather app with a car-themed user interface. Feel free to explore my projects, and let's connect to create something extraordinary!
